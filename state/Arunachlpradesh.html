Welcome to Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh, also known as the "Land of the Rising Sun," is a mesmerizing state in Northeast India, renowned for its majestic mountains, serene valleys, and rich tribal culture.


Tawang Monastery

India's largest monastery amid serene, scenic mountain landscapes.

Ziro Valley

Peaceful valley with pine hills and Apatani tribal culture.

Sela Pass

Snowy pass with lakes, connecting Tawang to other towns.

Namdapha National Park

Wildlife haven with red pandas, leopards, and rich biodiversity.

Dirang Valley

Hot springs, apple orchards, and ancient Dirang Dzong fort.

Bomdila

Monastery town with art, crafts, and mountain views.
